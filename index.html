FAQs

Frequently asked questions.

Why do you call yourselves a growth partner and not a typical agency?

+

Like our long-term investment thesis from our fund Hansa — we work on long-term horizons and become high-conviction growth partners with a small handful to give you the custom resources you need when you need them. We try and align incentives and act as a founding partner alongside your founding team. We are highly selective about who we work with, which is how we can guarantee success.

What kind of services do you provide?

+

Nozomi provides 360-degree crypto growth and marketing solutions, including GTM strategy development, fundraising strategy, paid ads services as well as social media marketing, content marketing, influencer marketing, community management, content writing, and more.

What's your pricing structure?

+

There is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question, as it will vary depending on the specific services you require and the size and scope of your project. However, as each campaign is unique and so is the cost, you can bundle and combine deals to achieve a better ROI and cost. Web3 is still in its infancy which means having generic deal terms alienates most ambitious projects. Some projects just need strategic advice, while others want to use us as their full-blown decentralised marketing team — from strategy all the way to Twitter/X management.

I am a CMO within my company. Will your team replace my job?

+

No. We prefer to work with senior internal marketing personnel to empower them to deliver on their KPIs and growth goals. Our team will manage the priorities and functions of the agency and ensure we are tracking towards your goals.

How many teams do you work with at the one time?

+

We do not work with more than 10 teams at any given time. We take partnership very seriously and we want to ensure that you get the attention and resources you deserve.

Does Nozomi provide funding?

+

Nozomi does not provide funding directly. Instead, we focus on driving tangible growth results with our strategy and executional support. We have a separate funding arm Hansa Capital that prioritises capital deployment and investments. We are happy to do introductions, and through our growth agency, we will get you to the stage where we'd be happy to look at the deal and introduce you to our network of capital partners.
